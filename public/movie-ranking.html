Atreyu's Movie Ranking List

My top 3 movies of all time!

Spies in Disguise

I love this movie not necessarily for the movie but because it was one of the first movies I watched with my favourite group of friends. It stars Tom Holland and Will Smith who were my favourite actors at the time, and with it being an animated comedy movie it was a great watch!

IP Man

I watched this movie when I studied Film in college. I think it was a cinematic masterpeice, the shots, music, frames, imagery, colours... everything was spectacular movie to watch and study.

Big Hero 6

I wasn't that young when I watched this movie, I was a teenager, but it feels like the movie of my childhood. I've watched it several times and absolutely love it, the idea that anyone could be a hero if they had the will and commitment was what stuck out to me the most, rather than having inate super powers or a special background, these were regular heros with big brains and a lot of passion who took it upon themselves to solve a problem. It's an inspiring and beautiful movie.
